Tribute Page

Linus Torvalds

Founder of Linux

  • Linus Torvalds was born on December 28, 1969, in Helsinki, Finland.
  • He grew up in a Finnish-speaking family and was named after Linus Pauling, a Nobel Prize-winning chemist
  • Torvalds created the Linux kernel in 1991 while studying at the University of Helsinki.
  • He released the first version of Linux, version 0.01, on September 17, 1991
  • Torvalds is credited with creating the Git version control system in 2005.
  • He has received numerous awards for his contributions to open-source software, including the Millennium Technology Prize in 2012
